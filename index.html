Meet Wink Optometrist Dr. Minal Patel

Dr. Minal Patel is a licensed therapeutic optometrist with experience in contact lenses, glaucoma, dry eyes, co-management of refractive (LASIK) surgery as well as cataract surgery.

Dr. Minal Patel earned her Bachelor of Science degree and Business Administration degree from Temple University and earned her Doctor of Optometry at Pennsylvania College of Optometry at Salus University in Jenkintown PA.

She completed a primary care externship at The Eye Institute in Philadelphia PA. She also advanced her knowledge and skills for diagnosing and treating ocular disease with Ocular Disease Externship at Omni Eye Care in Atlanta, GA.

She enjoys painting and working out in her spare time.

In addition to an on-site optometrist, Wink Eye Care and Optical has a wide array of frame and lens options for varying tastes and budgets. New and existing patients can book an eye exam using the easy, online scheduler on this website or call 215 935-6320.
